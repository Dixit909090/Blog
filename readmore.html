Air India ne ghatayi apni 15% international wide-body flights

June 19, 2025 | Thursday

Ahmedabad ke AI-171 crash ke baad, Air India ne apni international wide-body flights ko temporary roop se 15% tak ghatane ka faisla liya hai. Yeh faisla June 12 ke durghatna ke turant baad liya gaya jahan aircraft landing ke waqt runway se slip ho gaya tha.

Company ne kaha ki yeh kadam safety inspection ko aur majboot banane ke liye uthaya gaya hai. Ab mid-July tak sabhi wide-body aircrafts ki detailed checking hogi.
